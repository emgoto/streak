Setting up Streak

To get started, open a card and press the Enable button on the Streak settings pop-up in the card's side menu. You will need to do this per card that you want a streak on.

Once you've enabled Streak, you can track your habits using the calendar. If you don't need to do a habit every day, you can change the days a week number. Setting it to 0 allows you to never break your streak.

If you want to use Streak on the go, or you want more detailed notes about the habits you are tracking, you can also enable any comments you make on your card to count towards your streak.
