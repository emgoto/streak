Streak Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on the Streak Power-up for Trello.

Information we collect

Google Analytics

Streak uses Google Analytics, which stores cookies on your computer. The data that is collected includes your IP address and the time of your visit.

This information is not shared with third parties.

If you wish to opt out of Google Analytics, there is a browser add-on that will allow you to do so.

Your Trello data

Streak provides the optional feature where Trello's API is used to collect all comments on a card and see if they can count towards a streak, based off of the user's provided filter.

This data is not stored anywhere or shared with third parties, and is only used if the user has opted-in by authenticating with the Streak Power-Up.

Your consent

By using the Streak Trello Power-up, you consent to our privacy policy.
